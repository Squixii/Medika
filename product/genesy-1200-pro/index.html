GENESY 1200 Pro

• MY TRAINER: it shows the electrode position images directly on the display.
• RUN TIME: it permits to modify the contraction parameters during the stimulation.
• FAST MENU: for a more rapid navigation
• GRAPHIC LCD DISPLAY
• FUNCTIONAL RE-EDUCATION: the programs are designed to carry out a proper rehabilitation of the injured person through specific programs with square wave, denervated and interferential.
• MICROCURRENTS (MENS): Genesy 1200 offers the new and innovative “regenerative” and “therapeutic” currents, proposed for Sport, Beauty and, especially, Health treatments.
• PROGRAMMABLE: 15 free memories, only for EMS and TENS.
• 2+2 MODE: it permits to execute two different programs simultaneously.
• MULTI-USER: Genesy 1200 permits to share the stimulator among 25 users, keeping the setting and the preferences unchanged.
• AUTOMATIC MODE (AUTO STIM®): Genesy 1200 is the only stimulator that permits to execute a program in automatic mode with no need of intervening manually in the intensity regulation.
• MEMO MODE: Genesy 1200 permits to create a library of personalized programs depending on individual aims.
• SYNCROSTIM: it is possible to increase the intensity of the 4 channels simultaneously using only one button .
• LAST 10: Genesy 1200 stored the last executed programs. Therefore, they are available for a rapid and easy execution.
