RF Beauty 6000

Diathermy is a therapy that can produce energy and bring about a sensation of warmth in the treated area. The increase in temperature in the tissues gradually generates the restructuring of collagen superficially and deeply, thus making skin more compact and erasing the marks of time. Heat energy activates fibroblasts, which produce new collagen, thus regenerating tissues (collagenesis). The derma is therefore toned and the skin is smoother.
STRONG POINTS: diathermy can have extraordinary effects on the most widespread skin flaws, with no need to resort to aesthetic surgery or invasive methodologies. Moreover, the first results can be seen very soon after the first applications.
MULTI-FREQUENCY: Rf Beauty 6000 can emit different frequencies (from 400 kHz to 1 MHz). This technology has the outstanding advantage of reaching and working on different body tissues, from wrinkles to deep cellulite. Frequency of emission, is related to the depth of action (higher frequencies work in the surface).
MAIN INDICATIONS:
– face skin laxity
– Neck and cleavage skin laxity
– Abdomen laxity and adiposity
– Tights-gluteus-knees Laxity and adiposity
– Arms Laxity and adiposity
– Cellulite
– Stretch marks
– Scares
– Acne
– And wrinkles
