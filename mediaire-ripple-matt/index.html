The system will start the diagnostics and inflate the mattress. Simply wait until the LCD screen says “System Ready”. You can now put the system to use for a patient. When the patient is on, the system detects it instantly. The Auto-detection process will be completed in only a few minutes. The LCD will display: patient’s weight, automatic pressure setting according to the new weight and real-time pressure monitoring of the air cells. Unless you want to change the pressure setting or the operating mode. The caretaker can almost forget about the system operation. However, it is still a food idea to check the LCD display from time to time. The system is smart and powerful. If you want to adjust it, it comes with plenty of features. Such as: soft or firm, alternate or static, maximum inflation, pulsation and automatic fowler pressure boost. You can also set the pressure differentiation between A cells and B cells to match personal preferences. The system will record the usage time for the patient and it will be easy for you to keep the record for the treatment or billing purposes.
